Install the App

coffeerecipes.co is a web app. This means you can install the website on your device.

Installing the app means recipes are a tap away on your home screen.

iOS

  • Tap the share button

  • Tap "Add to Home Screen"

  • Tap "Add"

That's it! You can now access coffeerecipes.co from your home screen.

Android

  • Open Chrome
  • Go to coffeerecipes.co
  • Tap the menu button
  • Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  • Tap "Add"
